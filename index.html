About ProjectZERO

ProjectZERO is more than just a game - it's a passion project born from the love of classic Fortnite. Our mission is to preserve and celebrate the golden era of Fortnite, bringing back the memories and experiences that made millions fall in love with the game.

We've meticulously recreated the authentic feel of classic Fortnite while adding our own unique touches to enhance the experience for both pros and noobs alike.

Our Vision

ProjectZERO was conceived with a simple yet powerful vision: to recreate and preserve the magic of early Fortnite. We believe that gaming history should be preserved and experienced by future generations.

Our project aims to not just emulate, but enhance the classic Fortnite experience while maintaining its core essence. We're committed to regular updates, community feedback, and creating a sustainable platform for years to come.
